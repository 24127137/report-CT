\section{Phân tích yêu cầu hệ thống}
\subsection{Yêu cầu chức năng (Functional Requirements)}
\begin{enumerate}
    \item \textbf{User profiling}
    \begin{center}
        \begin{tabular}{|c|p{12cm}|}
        \hline
            ID & Requirement Description \\
        \hline
            FR-01   & The application must guide new users through an onboarding flow to select and their interests (e.g., Foodie, Hiking, History, Nightlife) to build a matching profile. \\
        \hline
            
            FR-02 & Users must be able to update their current travel status, location, and travel dates within their profile. \\
        \hline
            FR-03 & The application must support user registration and login via email, password\\
        \hline
        \end{tabular}
    \end{center}
    
    \item \textbf{Group Matching and Discovery}
    \begin{center}
        \begin{tabular}{|c|p{12cm}|}
        \hline
            ID & Requirement Description \\
        \hline
            FR-04 & The application must implement a matching algorithm to rank active groups based on shared destination, duration, travel plan, interests\\
        \hline
            FR-05 &
            The application must provide a 'Discover' feed that shows currently active groups matches.\\
        \hline
        \end{tabular}

    \end{center}
    \item \textbf{In-App Communication and AI Integration}
    \begin{center}
        \begin{tabular}{|c|p{12cm}|}
        \hline
            ID & Requirement Description \\
        \hline
            FR-06 &
            
            The application must provide persistent 1:1 and group chat functionality for connected users.\\
        \hline
            FR-07 &
            
            The application must integrate an AI Chatbot (using OpenAI or Gemini) accessible within the chat interface to answer travel-related questions, suggest activities, and help groups make decisions.\\
        \hline
        \end{tabular}

    \end{center}
\end{enumerate}

To ensure proper display, the table is formatted with automatic width adjustment and page breaking when it exceeds the page size.

\begin{center}
\begin{longtable}{|c|p{3cm}|p{5.5cm}|p{2cm}|p{3cm}|}
\hline
\textbf{Use Case ID} & \textbf{Use Case Name} & \textbf{Description} & \textbf{Actor(s)} & \textbf{Module Category} \\ \hline
UC01 & Sign In & User logs into the app using email and password. & User & Authentication \\ \hline
UC02 & Sign Up & User registers a new account by entering personal information. & User & Authentication \\ \hline
UC03 & Reset Password & User requests to reset a forgotten password via email. & User, Email Service & Authentication \\ \hline
UC04 & View Profile & User views their personal information stored in the database. & User & User Profile \\ \hline
UC05 & Edit Profile & User edits their profile information (name, gender, interests). & User & User Profile \\ \hline
UC06 & Change Language & User changes the app display language between English and Vietnamese. & User & Settings \\ \hline
UC07 & Create Travel Plan & User creates a new travel plan including destination, date, and places to visit. & User & Travel Plan \\ \hline
UC08 & Edit Travel Plan & User updates or modifies details of a saved travel plan. & User & Travel Plan \\ \hline
UC09 & View Travel Plan & User views saved travel plans from the database. & User & Travel Plan \\ \hline
UC10 & Group Matching & The system suggests users with similar interests and travel plans. & User, System & Group Matching \\ \hline
UC11 & Create Group & The host user creates a travel group and defines its details. & User (Host) & Group Management \\ \hline
UC12 & Delete Group & The host deletes an existing group and removes all members. & User (Host) & Group Management \\ \hline
UC13 & Leave Group & A member leaves a group they are part of. & User & Group Management \\ \hline
UC14 & Discard Member & The host removes a specific member from the group. & User (Host) & Group Management \\ \hline
UC15 & Match Users by Interest & The system automatically matches users based on shared interests and destinations. & System, User & Group Matching \\ \hline
UC16 & Send Join Request & User sends a request to join a travel group. & User & Group Management \\ \hline
UC17 & Receive Group Notification & User receives notifications for join requests or group updates. & User & Notification \\ \hline
UC18 & Accept Join Request & The host accepts a join request and adds the member to the group. & User (Host) & Group Management \\ \hline
UC19 & Reject Join Request & The host rejects a join request. & User (Host) & Group Management \\ \hline
UC20 & Start Voting Session & All group members vote to confirm destinations or travel plans. & User (All members) & Decision Voting \\ \hline
UC21 & View Group Info & User views group members and the shared travel plan. & User & Group Management \\ \hline
UC22 & AI Chatbot Suggestion & Chatbot suggests destinations and trip ideas. & User, Chatbot & Assistant \\ \hline

\end{center}
